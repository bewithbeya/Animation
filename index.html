Havanese Care

Havanese dogs need regular grooming to keep their long, silky coats healthy and free of tangles. Daily brushing is recommended, along with occasional baths.

They require moderate exercise such as short walks and playtime to stay happy and active. Havanese dogs also enjoy spending time with their owners and should not be left alone for long periods.

Routine vet visits, a balanced diet, and proper training help ensure a healthy and well-behaved Havanese. Positive reinforcement works best when training this intelligent and friendly breed.
